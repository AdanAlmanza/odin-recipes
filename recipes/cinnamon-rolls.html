Cinnamon Rolls

Description:

Cinnamon Rolls are soft, fluffy, and swirled with a sweet cinnamon-sugar filling. Topped with a creamy glaze, these rolls are the perfect indulgence for breakfast, brunch, or dessert. The rich buttery flavor and warm spices make them irresistible.

Ingredients:

  • 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar (for filling)
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened (for filling)
  • 1 cup powdered sugar (for glaze)
  • 2 tablespoons milk or heavy cream (for glaze)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (for glaze)

Steps:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, salt, and yeast.
  2. Warm the milk and water until slightly warm (not hot) and add to the dry ingredients along with melted butter and egg. Mix until combined.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a towel, and let it rise for 1-1.5 hours or until doubled in size.
  5. In a small bowl, mix together brown sugar and cinnamon for the filling.
  6. Roll the dough into a large rectangle and spread softened butter over it. Sprinkle the cinnamon-sugar mixture evenly over the butter.
  7. Tightly roll the dough into a log and cut it into 12 even pieces. Place the rolls in a greased baking dish.
  8. Cover the dish and let the rolls rise for another 30-45 minutes.
  9. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Bake the cinnamon rolls for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown.
  10. For the glaze, whisk together powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract until smooth. Drizzle over the warm rolls before serving.
